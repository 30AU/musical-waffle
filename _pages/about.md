Hoc in loco, quasi in foro litterarum, congregamur ut veritatem quaeramus, rationem colamus, atque sapientiam cum aliis communicemus. Neque enim solum nobis ipsis vivimus, sed etiam posteritati scribimus, ut studia nostra lumen afferant ad mentes futuras. 

Quid est enim tam dignum homine libero quam inquirere, disputare, docere? Hic igitur locus non ad vanam gloriam, sed ad honestam utilitatem comparatus est, ut omnes qui scientiam appetunt inveniant portum tranquillitatis et fontem doctrinae.

**English Translation:**

"In this place, as if in the forum of letters, we gather to seek truth, to cultivate reason, and to share wisdom with others. For we do not live for ourselves alone, but we also write for posterity, so that our studies may bring light to future minds. 

What is more worthy of a free person than to inquire, to debate, to teach? Therefore, this place has been established not for empty glory, but for honorable usefulness, so that all who desire knowledge may find a harbor of tranquility and a fountain of learning."
